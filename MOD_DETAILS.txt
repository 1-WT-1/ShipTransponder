Adds the ship's variant name or model to the OMS target list.

[u][b]Configuration:[/b][/u]

[b]Display Preference[/b]
[indent]* Sets whether to show the [i]Name[/i] or [i]Model[/i] first. The mod automatically falls back to the other option if the preferred one is not available.[/indent]

[b]Show Non-Hailable[/b]
[indent]* If enabled, the mod will attempt to display names for non-hailable targets, such as derelicts.[/indent]

[b]Show Untranslated Keys[/b]
[indent]* When enabled, displays the raw translation key instead of 'UNKNOWN' if translation is not found.[/indent]

[b]Dynamic Hybrid Naming[/b]
[indent]* If enabled, the Hybrid ship's identity will change frequently.[/indent]
